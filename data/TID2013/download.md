# TID2013 (Tampere Image Database 2013)

* **Summary**: A comprehensively massive synthetic dataset. It features 25 reference images evaluated against 24 distinct distortion types, each at 5 levels of severity, resulting in 3,000 highly diverse distorted images.
* **Link**: [TID2013 Database](http://www.ponomarenko.info/tid2013.htm)
* **Direct download**: <https://huggingface.co/datasets/xmba15/TID2013>
