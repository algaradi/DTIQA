# LIVEC (LIVE In the Wild Image Quality Challenge Database)

* **Summary**: Comprises 1,162 authentically distorted images captured from diverse mobile devices in unconstrained, real-world environments. Rated extensively by crowd-sourcing.
* **Link**: [LIVEC Database](https://live.ece.utexas.edu/research/ChallengeDB/index.html)
* **Direct download**: <https://utexas.app.box.com/v/ChallengeDB-release>
