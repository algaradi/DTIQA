# KonIQ-10k (Konstanz Artificially Distorted Image Database)

* **Summary**: A massive, ecologically valid dataset consisting of 10,073 in-the-wild images sampled from the immense YFCC100m database, annotated through large-scale crowdsourcing to ensure exceptionally high statistical reliability.
* **Link**: [KonIQ-10k Database](https://database.mmsp-kn.de/koniq-10k-database.html)
