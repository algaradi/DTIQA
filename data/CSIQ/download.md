# CSIQ (Categorical Image Quality Database)

* **Summary**: Comprises 30 original reference images, distorted across 6 types of synthetic degradation (JPEG, JPEG2000, Gaussian Blur, Gaussian White Noise, Gaussian Pink Noise, Contrast Change) at 4-5 distortion levels, yielding 866 distorted images.
* **Link**: [CSIQ Database](https://qualinet.github.io/databases/image/categorical_image_quality_csiq_database/)
* **Direct download**: <https://s2.smu.edu/~eclarson/csiq.html>
